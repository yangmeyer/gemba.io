The Gemba Story

Over the past five years of creating mobile apps, I have worked with many different cross-functional teams. Over and over, I noticed the same challenges with how we, as designers and developers, work together.

I believe that deeper collaboration makes for better apps. With Gemba, I hope to bring designers and developers closer together through better tooling. Gemba allows designers to work on the living product itself – the “real place”, or “Gemba” in Japanese.

If you have any questions or comments, say hi!

—Yang Meyer, Stockholm
