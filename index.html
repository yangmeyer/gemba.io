Detect problematic assets

Gemba helps spot problematic assets before they are integrated into the app. It warns you about implausible and mismatching asset dimensions: e.g. blurry 43×43 Retina asset, mismatching Retina/non-Retina assets, differing dimensions from previous version.

Be empowered

Integrate assets into the app yourself – no developer required. Take responsibility over your design work where it matters: in the live app.
