Dear app creator,

In the past four years of creating mobile apps, I have worked with many different designers on different teams. All teams faced similar challenges in the way designers and developers work together.

Gemba is an attempt to bring designers and developers closer together through better tooling, in the belief that more intimate collaboration makes for better apps.

—Yang Meyer, Stockholm

Gemba helps you…

  • Iterate painlessly

    Integrate asset updates whenever you want. No developer required.

  • Detect problems

    Gemba warns you about implausible and mismatching asset dimensions.

  • Be empowered

    Take responsibility over your design work where it matters: in the app.
